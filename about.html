About JB Fireside

More than a food truck - it's a tradition, a community, and a taste of home.

Our Story

JB Fireside started with one vision: to bring the bold, smoky flavors of Caribbean cooking straight to the streets of Killeen. With recipes passed down and refined over generations, every dish is served with love, fire, and authenticity.

Milestones

2023

JB Fireside food truck first hit the road, serving authentic Caribbean street food.

2024

Featured in the grand opening of the food truck park "The Taste of Killeen", bringing people out to have fun for a while and try out the menu.

2025

Still growing, still cooking, and still serving plates fille with fire and passion.

Our Mission

To share the flavors of the Caribbean while building community, one meal at a time. Whether it's Jerk Chicken, Oxtail, or Curry Goat, our goal is to make every bite taste like home.
